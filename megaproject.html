Founder Of SRM

SRM Full Form: SRM stands for Sri Ramaswamy Memorial Institute of Science and Technology; it is located in Kattankulathur, Tamil Nadu near Chennai. SRM was founded in the year 1985 with a total of seven campuses. Out of seven three are located in Tamil Nadu, one in NCR Delhi.
